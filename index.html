About

Sorority Welcoming is about a venue mainly created for women. However, it also can be rented as a party venue for all genders.

This venue will offer different services:

  • Place for women to meet and share knowledge
  • Place for women to work
  • Place for women to entertain or chill
  • Place for parties

Topics

Share knowledge

Help other women to learn new skills

Co-working

Work and use the facilities such as printer, meeting room, office

Entertainment

Show your artistry to other women, or just chill

Parties

Rent the venue for your wedding or other parties. use the kitchen for catering
